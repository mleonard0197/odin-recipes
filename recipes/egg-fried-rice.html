Uncle Roger Egg Fried Rice

Uncle Roger supremely simple egg fried recipe!

Ingredients:

  • Peanut oil
  • Garlic cloves, crushed
  • Shallot
  • Egg
  • Red Chili
  • Day old rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • MSG... fuiyoh

Steps:

  1. Coat pan with oil over medium heat.
  2. Once it starts smoking, pour out excess oil and add in shallot and garlic and fry.
  3. Add in egg. Once it's almost cooked, add in cooked rice.
  4. After it's mixed, add in soy sauce and sesame oil and flatten into the rice.
  5. Add in MSG and red chili and all done.
