Delicious Chicken Adobo

The greatest chicken recipe of all time? Maybe so....It's also one of the easiest chicken recipes of all time.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken drumsticks
  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 20 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • Black pepper
  • 1 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce, sweet
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce, dark
  • At least 1 tbsp black peppercorn
  • 4 bay leaves
  • Rice for serving

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, add in all the chicken (thighs and drumsticks) and the heavily crushed cloves of garlic.
  2. To that add a fresh crack of black pepper, black peppercorns, palm sugar, unseasoned rice vinegar, dark soy sauce, regular soy sauce, filtered water, and toss together and add in bay leaves.
  3. Cover and marinate for 2 hours, or overnight.
  4. Set a braiser or dutch oven over med-high heat and coat with oil.
  5. Sear all chicken pieces for 2-3 minutes per side. Set aside after browning to avoid overcrowding.
  6. Add marination to pan, and increase head to med-high. Add in chicken pieces in layers and bring to a boil.
  7. Once it reaches a boil, reduce heat to low, cover, and let simmer for 35 minutes. After, uncover, flip the pieces, and cover again for 25-35 minutes.
  8. Play chicken on a place with your rice, and cover according to taste with your pan sauce.
